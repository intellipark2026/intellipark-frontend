About IntelliPark

IntelliPark is a Smart QR-Based Parking Navigation System with Real-Time Slot Monitoring and Online Reservations — a capstone project developed by Computer Engineering students from STI College Caloocan. The system is designed to modernize and simplify the parking experience using Internet of Things (IoT) technology, enabling users to reserve parking slots, navigate directly to them, and pay through cashless QR-based transactions.

Our Mission

To revolutionize parking management by integrating intelligent automation, real-time monitoring, and user-friendly digital tools — reducing congestion, saving time, and promoting sustainable urban mobility.

Our Vision

To become a leading smart parking innovation that enhances the efficiency, accessibility, and convenience of parking systems across cities and commercial establishments in the Philippines.

System Overview

IntelliPark combines the power of IoT, sensors, and web-based technologies to deliver a seamless parking experience. Ultrasonic sensors monitor slot availability, while kiosks equipped with touchscreens and QR scanners handle on-site check-ins and payments. Users can reserve parking online, receive a digital QR code, and be guided directly to their assigned slot — all synchronized with real-time updates in the Firebase Realtime Database. This integration minimizes human error, streamlines entry and exit processes, and enhances security.
